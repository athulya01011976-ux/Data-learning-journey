## (6) What are the peak order days and peak order hours?



### Query Summary
Analyzed order volume by day of the week and hour of the day to identify the peak ordering based on order count.



### Insight
- Order activity peaks during evening hours (between 14:00 and 22:00)
- higher order volumes are observed on Sunday, Tuesday, and Thursday compared to other days



### Business Impact
Identifying peak ordering periods enables better staffing, delivery scheduling, and resource planning to meet the higher demand and reduce delivery delays.



### Recommendation

- increase the delivery capacity during evening hours on high-demand days.
